About Our Academy

Eric Table Tennis Academy features state-of-the-art facilities with Butterfly table tennis equipment sponsorship, 17 Butterfly competition-grade tables, ITTF-approved sports flooring, clean and tidy facilities, high ceilings, and ample space suitable for table tennis enthusiasts and professional athletes at all levels.

We create an excellent practice atmosphere and sports environment for children, allowing them to experience the charm of table tennis outside of study time, while cultivating more excellent table tennis players. For adult table tennis enthusiasts, we provide excellent facilities, clean and tidy sports venues, and a healthy sports atmosphere.
